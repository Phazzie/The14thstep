# Product Guidelines

## Visual Identity
- **Tone:** Gritty realism with dark humor. The aesthetic should be grounded, intimate, and evoke a late-night "basement meeting" vibe.
- **Color Palette:** Dark mode focused (grays, blacks) with amber/orange accents for warmth and emphasis. Avoid clinical blues or sterile whites.
- **Visuals & Avatars:** Abstract & Minimal. Use simple colored circles with initials (M, H, Me) and subtle glows to focus attention on the voices and text, avoiding uncanny valley issues with generated faces.

## User Interaction
- **Gamified:** Users earn badges or streaks for participation, encouraging daily usage through rewards. While the tone is serious, the mechanics should reinforce consistency ("Keep Coming Back").
- **Friction:** Low friction entry. The app prioritizes getting the user into a meeting quickly. Setup is minimal, passing is easy ("Just listening"), and there are no heavy onboarding walls.

## Voice & Copy
- **System Messages:** Realistic & Gritty. Avoid "touchy-feely shit" or corporate polish. Use direct, street-smart language that feels authentic to recovery rooms (e.g., "Keep coming back," "The room held space for you," "Don't leave before the miracle").
- **Error Messages:** Blunt but helpful. Avoid generic tech jargon.

## Success Metrics
- **Primary:** **Return Rate.** The user feels accepted and "heard," leading them to come back tomorrow or in a moment of need (measured by retention).
- **Secondary:** **Interaction Depth.** Users engage deeply, responding to prompts and expanding shares (measured by tokens generated).
