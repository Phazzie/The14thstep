## User Persistence
- **Full User Accounts:** Users must sign up/login. The system permanently tracks their clean time, attendance history, past shares, and evolving relationships with AI characters.

## Simulation Mechanics
- **Dynamic & Realistic:** Meetings vary in size (8-15 participants). A core group of "regulars" (Marcus, Meechie, etc.) appears frequently, mixed with a rotating pool of recurring characters and occasional newcomers.

## Emotional Intelligence & Tone
- **Authentic & Human:** AI characters respond like real people, maintaining character consistency even during heavy or crisis moments. They do not break character or revert to robotic/clinical responses.

## Character Memory & Relationships
- **Deep Memory:** AI characters remember past shares, inside jokes ("Meechie's chair"), specific conflicts, and relationships with other characters AND returning users, referencing these naturally over time.

## Meeting Formats
- **Open Discussion (Core):** The standard format where a topic is selected and the floor is open for sharing.
- **Step/Literature Study:** Focused meetings based on specific recovery texts or steps (e.g., "Step 1", "Tradition 3").

## Reference Implementation
- **Source:** React artifact provided by user.
- **Key Characters:** Marcus (The Chair), Heather (The Queen Returned), Meechie (The Truth), Gemini (The War Inside), Gypsy (The Runner), Chrystal (The Proof).
- **Core Logic:** Character-specific prompting, crisis detection keywords, "empty chair" ritual, distinct "phases" (setup, intro, topic, discussion, closing).
